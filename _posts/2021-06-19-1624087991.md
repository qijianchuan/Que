解决方案：

    
    
    <dependency>
       <groupId>org.testng</groupId>
       <artifactId>testng</artifactId>
       <version>1.0-SNAPSHOT</version>
       <scope>test</scope>
    
    </dependency>
    

修改为

    
    
    <dependency>
        <groupId>org.testng</groupId>
        <artifactId>testng</artifactId>
        <version>6.14.2</version>
        <scope>test</scope>
    </dependency>
